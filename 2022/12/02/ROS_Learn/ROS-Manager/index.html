launch文件

launch文件

用于启动多个节点，利用launch文件提高效率

launch标签

所有的标签都是在下的根标签

  • launch

    <launch deprecated="notification">

</launch>

  • node

    pkg="package_name"
type="executable file"
name="name of node"
args=""
machine="name of machine"
respawn="true/false"
required="true/false"
ns="namespace"
clear_params="true/false"
output="log/screen"

  • include

    <include file="" ns="" />

  • remap

    <node pkg="turtlesim" type="turtlesim_node" name="my_turtle" output="screen"/>
    <remap from="/turtle1/cmd_vel" to="/cmd_vel"/>
</node>

  • param

    name="namespace"
value="value"
type="data type"
<param name="param_node" type="double" value="1.09"/>

  • rosparam(先执行)

    command="load/dump/delete"
file="file path"
param=""
ns="namespace"

  • group

    ns="namespace"
clear_params="true/false"
<launch>
    <group ns="turle1">
        
    </group>
</launch>

  • arg

    name=""
defalut=""
value=""
doc=""

echo $ROS_PACKAGE_PATH

应当尽量避免功能包重名
